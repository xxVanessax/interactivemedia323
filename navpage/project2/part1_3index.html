Poem in which...

                                  I have wisdom.

                                                                          I have a father.


                                          Poem in which I care.


    I am from another country.



                                        I Spanish.

                                                                       flowers are important.


        I make pretty gestures.


                                          I am a Deceptacon.


                                                                                      I am a novelist.

                    I use trash.


                                                              I am a baby.


     I swaddle.
                                             I bathe.

                                                                                        I am a box.


  its face is everything.


                                                                         faces are everywhere.


                                 I swear.


                                                                 I take an oath.


            I make a joke.


                                                                                                                                     ...I can't move.
