POEM

in which I have wisdom.
in which I have a father.
in which I care.
in which I am from another country.
in which I Spanish.
in which flowers are important.
in which I make pretty gestures.
in which I am a Deceptacon.
in which I am a novelist.
in which I use trash.
in which I am a baby.
in which I swaddle.
in which I bathe.
in which I am a box.
in which its face is everything.
in which faces are everywhere.
in which I swear.
in which I take an oath.
in which I make a joke.
in which I can't move.
